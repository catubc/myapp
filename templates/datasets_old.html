Below we provide simulated, in vitro and synthetic in vivo data. Detailed probe layouts can be obtained by clicking on each probe figure and we currently provide parameter files (.prb) for Klusta-Kwik as well as a simplified time-spike-file format (.tsf) which contains the channel layout.

Please contact us for help loading and formating the data for your particular spike sorting software.
